# software security testing

- confidentiality: does the app keep private data private?
- integrity: can the data from the app be trusted and verified?
- authentication: does the app verify you are who you say you are?
- authorization: does your app properly limit user privileges?
- availability: can an attacker take the app offline?
- non-repudiation: does the app keep records of events?
